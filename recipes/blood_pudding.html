Blood Pudding

Blood pudding, or "boudin noir" if you're feeling fancy.

It's the deep, dark blood.

Ingredients

  • Pig's blood.
  • Pork fat. Greasy, slippery.
  • Onions, finely chopped. They sting your eyes.
  • Stale bread or breadcrumbs

Recipes instructions

  1. Start with the onions, frying them until they're translucent.
  2. Mix the blood, the pork fat, and those onions in a bowl.
  3. Throw in the breadcrumbs or stale bread. They'll soak up the blood.
  4. Shape them into whatever form feels right. Cook them until they're done, but not too done. There's a fine line between perfection and disaster.

Blood Pudding. Pour yourself a drink, eat, and reflect.
